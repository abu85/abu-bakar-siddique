Hi, I'm Abu Bakar Siddique, a bioinformatician and molecular ecologist.
My research integrates an ecological perspective with metagenomics. I conducted multiple research projects, including TanAsp (mycobiome and metabolites in European Aspen), Sweden Biodiversity in Time and Space (SweBITS) through meta-genomics, mycoviruses, leaf-inhabiting endophytes of European beech trees, and plant-fungi-insect interaction in Swedish Aspen.
